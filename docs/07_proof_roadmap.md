# Proof Roadmap

This repository is a computational sandbox, not a proof.

A proof-oriented program would need to address:

- rigorous construction of four-dimensional quantum Yang-Mills theory,
- gauge-invariant observable definitions,
- reflection positivity or another route to a physical Hilbert space,
- transfer matrix or Hamiltonian spectral control,
- continuum limit,
- infinite-volume limit,
- proof that the lowest non-vacuum gauge-invariant energy is strictly positive.

Finite-lattice experiments can organize intuition and test hypotheses. They do not by themselves establish the continuum theorem.
